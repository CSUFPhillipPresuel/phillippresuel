Privacy Policy For SkinByAnaLaura:

SkinByAnaLaura does not ask for any sensitive information because we value your feeling of privacy and security. We do not even go as far as to ask for an Email to have on file. We hope to build your trust by being completely transparent about how seriously we take the exchange of information. Our forum is public to all users, can be viewed by all users, and any user can share to it but it is completely optional. We allow and encourage anonymity because of this fact, the purpose of our forum is to feel comfortable asking whatever questions you may have and we take this seriously. We encourage you use the secure Emails listed on SkinByAnaLaura or this website if you need to discuss more sensitive information. If you have any questions regarding our procedures or policies, please contact us at my personal Email "phillippresuel@gmail.com".
